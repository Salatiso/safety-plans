About Safety First OHS Tools

Welcome to Safety First OHS Tools! Our mission is to make Occupational Health and Safety (OHS) compliance simple, fast, and accessible for everyone in South Africa, especially in construction. We provide free, easy-to-use tools that help you meet legal requirements and keep your workplace safe.

Who We Are

Safety First OHS Tools was created by Salatiso Lonwabo Mdeni, a passionate advocate for workplace safety in South Africa. Inspired by the need to simplify OHS compliance, this site brings together practical tools to help businesses, contractors, and workers stay safe and meet regulations like the Occupational Health and Safety Act 85 of 1993 and Construction Regulations 2014.
