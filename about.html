About SafetyHelp

Welcome to SafetyHelp, a platform born from over 20 years of my experience in the health and safety industry. I’m Salatiso Lonwabo Mdeni, and I’ve dedicated my career to enhancing workplace and community safety, starting with manual tools that were often cumbersome and limited to professionals. My vision has always been to create a future where OHS tools are accessible to everyone, automated where possible, integrated with risk management systems, and enhanced by AI to foster safer environments.

With extensive experience in both private and public sectors—including roles at Anglo Platinum, Metrorail, and Absa—and running my OHS and risk management consultancy since 2012, I’ve seen firsthand the impact of effective safety practices. SafetyHelp is the culmination of this journey, inspired by my books, Safety First: The Essentials of OHS Plans and Safety First: Essentials for Your OHS Career Journey, to empower businesses, professionals, and communities worldwide.

My Journey in Health and Safety

My passion for safety began at Kiriyatswane Secondary School in Embalenhle, where the scent of a nearby Sasol plant sparked my curiosity about health and safety. I pursued Environmental Health at Pretoria Technikon (now Tshwane University of Technology), starting my career as a Junior Environmental Health Practitioner in East London. From there, I gained hands-on experience in mining with Anglo Platinum, managed safety programs at Metrorail, and led emergency response initiatives at Absa.

Since 2012, I’ve run my own OHS and risk management consultancy, working across sectors to improve safety standards. My books, Safety First: The Essentials of OHS Plans and Safety First: Essentials for Your OHS Career Journey, reflect my commitment to making safety accessible. SafetyHelp embodies this vision, bringing my expertise to a digital platform to create safer workplaces and communities for all.

Our Core Values

The values that guide SafetyHelp stem from my personal philosophy, reflected in the acronym SALATISO—Safety, Assurance, Learning, Adaptability, Training, Integrity, Solutions, and Operations. These principles shape everything we do.

  • 🛡️ Safety: Prioritizing the well-being of every individual.
  • ✅ Assurance: Ensuring confidence through reliable safety practices.
  • 📚 Learning: Committing to continuous improvement and education.
  • 🔄 Adaptability: Embracing change to meet evolving safety needs.
  • 🎓 Training: Empowering users with knowledge and skills.
  • 🤝 Integrity: Upholding honesty and ethical standards in all we do.
  • 💡 Solutions: Providing practical, innovative tools for safety challenges.
  • ⚙️ Operations: Streamlining processes for efficient safety management.
