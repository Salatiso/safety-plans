About Us

Safety First OHS Tools is your go-to platform for simplifying Occupational Health and Safety (OHS) compliance in South Africa. Inspired by Salatiso Lonwabo Mdeni and the acclaimed Safety First book series, we’re here to make safety accessible, practical, and community-driven.

Our Story

Created by Salatiso Lonwabo Mdeni, this platform builds on the success of the Safety First books—A Comprehensive Guide and A Step-by-Step Guide. Our mission is to empower OHS professionals, clients, and newcomers with automated tools, expert resources, and a vibrant community. From construction sites to office spaces, we’re making safety smarter and simpler.

Our Mission

We believe OHS should be approachable for everyone—not just experts. That’s why we offer tools to streamline compliance, a directory to connect you with pros, and a community to share ideas. Whether you’re generating H&S specs or seeking a mentor, we’ve got you covered.
