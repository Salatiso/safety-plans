Occupational Health and Safety (OHS) System

Ensure compliance with South African OHS legislation and international standards like ISO 45001 by compiling a comprehensive OHS management system for your organization. Use our interactive wizard to create essential documents, including the OHS Management System Manual, policies, procedures, and supporting forms. Customize templates with your company details and download them as PDFs, all while aligning with ISO 9000 quality management principles.

Download a Sample OHS Management System Manual: Click Here

OHS Act Obligations

Section 7: Requires a CEO-signed Health and Safety Policy.

Section 8: Ensure a safe, risk-free workplace.

Section 13: Provide OHS training to employees.

COIDA Obligations

Section 24: Report work-related injuries.

Registration: Register with the Compensation Fund.

Records: Retain incident records for audits.

ISO 45001 Requirements

Clause 6.1.2: Conduct hazard identification and risk assessment.

Clause 8.2: Develop emergency response plans.

Clause 9.2: Perform regular audits and inspections.
