Baseline Risk Assessment Tool

This tool helps you identify and assess workplace risks as outlined in Safety First: The Essentials of OHS Plans. Follow the steps below to conduct a baseline risk assessment for your organization.

Step 1: Identify Hazards

Walk through your workplace and list potential hazards, such as machinery, chemicals, or ergonomic issues.

Step 2: Assess Risks

For each hazard, evaluate the likelihood and severity of potential incidents. Use a risk matrix to prioritize risks.

Step 3: Implement Controls

Develop control measures to mitigate identified risks, such as engineering controls, administrative controls, or personal protective equipment (PPE).
