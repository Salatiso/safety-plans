Welcome to Safety First OHS Tools

Navigating Occupational Health and Safety (OHS) compliance in South Africa is critical for businesses to ensure the safety of workers, visitors, and the public. The Occupational Health and Safety Act, 1993 (Act No. 85 of 1993), along with its regulations, such as the Construction Regulations, 2014, sets out clear legal obligations for employers, contractors, and clients. Compliance not only protects lives but also prevents costly penalties, project delays, and legal action.

At Safety First OHS Tools, we provide resources to simplify compliance, empowering you to meet legal requirements efficiently and effectively. Whether you're a business owner, contractor, or safety professional, our tools are designed to support you at every step.
