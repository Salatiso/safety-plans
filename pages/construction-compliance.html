Construction Health and Safety

Ensure compliance with construction health and safety regulations using Salatiso OHS Tools. Our resources help you manage risks, comply with legal requirements, and protect workers on-site, drawing from best practices outlined in "Safety First: The Essentials of OHS Plans."

Client Obligations

Construction Regulation 5(1)(a): Prepare a Baseline Risk Assessment (BRA) identifying project hazards.

Construction Regulation 5(1)(b): Provide a Health & Safety Specification outlining project-specific requirements.

Construction Regulation 5(1)(l): Approve the Contractor‚Äôs Health & Safety Plan before work begins.

Contractor Obligations

Construction Regulation 7(1)(a): Develop a Health & Safety Plan based on the Client‚Äôs specification.

Construction Regulation 7(1)(b): Maintain an on-site Health & Safety File with all required documents.

Construction Regulation 9: Conduct task-specific Hazard Identification and Risk Assessments (HIRAs).

H&S Professional Obligations

Construction Regulation 5(5): Agents (PrCHSA) manage H&S on behalf of the Client, ensuring compliance.

Construction Regulation 8(5): Officers (CHSO) assist Contractors in implementing and monitoring H&S systems.

SACPCMP Guidelines: Must maintain registration and CPD requirements for professional roles.
