% Content-Specific Sections
\section{Purpose}
The purpose of this manual is to:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Provide a framework for managing OHS risks and opportunities.
    \item Ensure compliance with OHSA 85 of 1993 and SANS 45001.
    \item Promote a safe and healthy workplace for all employees, contractors, and visitors.
    \item Facilitate continual improvement in OHS performance.
\end{itemize}

\section{Scope}
This manual applies to all \clientName\ operations, workplaces, and activities in South Africa, covering employees, contractors, and visitors.

\section{OHS Management System Structure}
\subsection{Leadership and Commitment}
Top management, led by the CEO ({{CEO_NAME}}), commits to:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Establishing and maintaining the OHS Management System.
    \item Providing resources for OHS initiatives.
    \item Promoting a culture of safety and compliance.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Planning}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Risk Assessments:} Conduct HIRAs to identify and control risks (SANS 45001 Clause 6.1).
    \item \textbf{Legal Compliance:} Maintain a Legal Register to ensure adherence to OHSA and regulations.
    \item \textbf{OHS Objectives:} Set measurable objectives, tracked via the OHS Objectives Tracker.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Support}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Training:} Provide training on OHS policies, procedures, and legal requirements.
    \item \textbf{Communication:} Facilitate internal and external OHS communication per the OHS Communication Policy.
    \item \textbf{Documentation:} Manage OHS documentation and records per the OHS Documentation Policy.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Operation}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Control Measures:} Implement controls per the OHS Control Measures Policy.
    \item \textbf{Emergency Preparedness:} Maintain emergency response plans per the OHS Emergency Management Policy.
    \item \textbf{Contractor Management:} Manage contractors per the OHS Contractor Management Policy.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Performance Evaluation}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Audits and Inspections:} Conduct regular audits per the OHS Audit and Inspection Policy.
    \item \textbf{Performance Reviews:} Monitor KPIs and objectives per the OHS Performance Review Policy.
    \item \textbf{Incident Management:} Investigate and report incidents per the OHS Incident Management Policy.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Improvement}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Address non-conformities and implement corrective actions (SANS 45001 Clause 10.2).
    \item Continually improve the OHS Management System based on audit findings, incident analyses, and management reviews.
\end{itemize}

\section{Legal and Standard References}
This manual is guided by:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Occupational Health and Safety Act 85 of 1993.
    \item SANS 45001: Occupational Health and Safety Management Systems.
    \item Relevant regulations (e.g., Construction Regulations 2014, General Safety Regulations).
\end{itemize]

\section{Manual Review}
This manual will be reviewed at least every two years or as necessitated by changes in legislation, operations, or OHS performance.
