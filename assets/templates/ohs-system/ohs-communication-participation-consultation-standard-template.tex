% Content-Specific Sections
\section{Purpose}
The purpose of this standard is to:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Define procedures for OHS communication, participation, and consultation.
    \item Establish responsibilities for implementing these processes.
    \item Ensure compliance with OHSA 85 of 1993 and SANS 45001.
    \item Enhance worker involvement in OHS decision-making.
\end{itemize}

\section{Scope}
This standard applies to all \clientName\ operations, employees, contractors, and stakeholders in South Africa.

\section{Definitions}
Refer to the \clientName\ OHS Communication, Participation, and Consultation Policy for key definitions. Additional definitions include:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{H&S Committee:} A group of management and worker representatives responsible for OHS consultation.
    \item \textbf{Communication Plan:} A structured approach to sharing OHS information.
\end{itemize}

\section{Legal and Standard References}
This standard is guided by:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Occupational Health and Safety Act 85 of 1993 (Sections 17, 18).
    \item SANS 45001: Occupational Health and Safety Management Systems (Clause 5.4).
\end{itemize]

\section{OHS Communication, Participation, and Consultation Process}

\subsection{Communication Procedures}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Develop an OHS Communication Plan, specifying channels (e.g., meetings, emails, posters).
    \item Ensure information is accessible in multiple languages (e.g., English, Zulu, Xhosa).
    \item Share OHS updates, incident reports, and policy changes promptly.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Worker Participation}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Encourage participation in HIRAs, incident investigations, and safety committees.
    \item Provide platforms (e.g., suggestion boxes, digital apps) for workers to report hazards.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Consultation Processes}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Hold regular H&S committee meetings to discuss OHS matters.
    \item Consult workers before implementing new OHS policies or controls.
    \item Document consultation outcomes and actions taken.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Training}
Provide training on effective OHS communication and consultation per the OHS Training Policy.

\section{Record Keeping}
Maintain records of communication plans, H&S committee minutes, and consultation outcomes for at least 5 years.

\section{Standard Review}
This standard will be reviewed at least every two years or as necessitated by changes in legislation or operational needs.
