Construction Compliance Tool

This tool helps you ensure compliance with construction regulations as outlined in the Occupational Health and Safety Act (OHSA) and Construction Regulations. Follow the steps below to develop a comprehensive Construction OHS Plan for your project.

Statutory Requirements

Ensure you have the following in place:

  • Letter of Good Standing: Verify your registration with the Compensation for Occupational Injuries and Diseases Act (COID).
  • Notification of Construction Work: Submit notification to the Department of Employment and Labour as per the Construction Regulations.

Risk Assessments

Conduct a baseline risk assessment to identify hazards specific to your construction site, such as machinery hazards, chemical exposure, and slips/trips/falls.

Emergency Management

Develop an Emergency Response Plan to address potential incidents like fires, medical emergencies, and chemical spills. Include evacuation procedures and emergency contact numbers.

Induction and Training

Implement induction training for all workers entering the site, covering site-specific hazards, safety procedures, and emergency protocols. Conduct regular toolbox talks to reinforce safety awareness.
