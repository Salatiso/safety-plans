Understand South African OHS Compliance

The Occupational Health and Safety Act, 1993 (Act No. 85 of 1993) mandates employers to ensure a safe working environment. Section 8(1) states: “Every employer shall provide and maintain, as far as is reasonably practicable, a working environment that is safe and without risk to the health of his employees.”
